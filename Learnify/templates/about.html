We aim to support self learners around the world

When we are learning new topics using internet, there are tons of sources that we can look at. This is a significant benefit we gained by the innovation of internet. However, often time not all the websites are relevant for what we want to learn. Learnify counts on the fact that there were probably certain number of people who learned the same topic of interest as yours, and they know the best websites that solves your problem. This is the platform that allows people to create their own list of URL's that are suitable for learning a certain topic.
